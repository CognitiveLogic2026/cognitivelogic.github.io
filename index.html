What is Cognitive Logic?

Cognitive Logic is a research-driven ecosystem focused on building AI-native knowledge infrastructures that integrate semantic reasoning, ethical governance, sustainability intelligence, and decentralized digital identity.

It represents a post-LLM cognitive architecture designed to support explainable AI, trusted automation, and large-scale multi-agent intelligence.
