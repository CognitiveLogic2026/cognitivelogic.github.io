QEN Formula
Transparent · Open

Same formula applied to all sectors. Public weights, public calculation, fully verifiable.

QEN Score = (Vs × 0.40) + (Va × 0.35) + (Vt × 0.25)
Vs — Social (40%): Worker welfare, representation, inclusion, fair wages
Va — Environmental (35%): Supply chain km0, waste, energy, circular economy
Vt — Territorial (25%): Local rootedness, community presence, Bologna ecosystem
